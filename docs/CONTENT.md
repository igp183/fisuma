# Adding Content

You don't need to touch any code to add or update content on the site.
Everything goes through the Supabase dashboard.

## Getting access

Ask a maintainer to invite you to the Supabase project.

## Accessing the dashboard

1. Go to [supabase.com](https://supabase.com) and sign in
2. Open the **FISUMa** project
3. Click **Table Editor** in the left sidebar

## Adding content

Each type of content (events, posts, team members, etc.) has its own table.
To add a new entry, open the relevant table, click **Insert row**, fill in the fields, and click **Save**.

If a field is unclear, ask a maintainer.

## What not to touch

- Do not edit or delete tables or columns in the **Database** section
- Do not change anything under **Authentication** or **Storage** unless you know what you're doing
- If something looks wrong, contact a maintainer instead of trying to fix it directly
