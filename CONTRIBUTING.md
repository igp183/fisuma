# Contributing to FISUMa

Thanks for wanting to help. This guide will point you in the right direction depending on what you want to do.

Don't hesitate to contribute however you can, but following the guidelines on this Markdown is appreciated.

---

## What do you want to do?

**Fix a bug, add a feature, or change the design**
- Read [docs/DEVELOPMENT.md](docs/DEVELOPMENT.md)

**Change the database structure**
- Read [docs/DATABASE.md](docs/DATABASE.md)

**Add or fix a translation**
- Read [docs/TRANSLATIONS.md](docs/TRANSLATIONS.md)

**Add an event, news post, or other content**
- Read [docs/CONTENT.md](docs/CONTENT.md)

---

## General principles

- Try to keep PRs small and focused, one PR for one objective.
- If you're unsure about something, don't hesitate to ask first.
- Content changes (events, posts) don't need a PR, they go directly through the Supabase dashboard

Have a question? Open an issue or reach out to a member of FISUMa.
